Digital Billboard

Digital billboards are primarily used for advertising, but they can also serve public service purposes. Advertising agencies can update the advertisements on these billboards remotely, by using the wireless cellular phone network to access the billboard computer. A designer produces the digital advertisement on a computer, which can then be uploaded to any number of displays. Right now, the billboard are walk into people's lives.

Pedestals

Pedestals are usually based on the ground or the on the side of the structures,and it provide protection to distribution equipment industries.These pedestals are used for splicing, terminating and distributing copper. These cabinets and pedestals are core parts of Enable’s fibre broadband network and are critical to providing fibre broadband services to the Wellington community.
